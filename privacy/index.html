Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-20

MenuIQ ("we", "the app") is built to read restaurant menus and rank dishes for your goal. This policy explains exactly what data the app handles and why. We keep it short because we collect very little.

What we process

  • Menu photos. When you scan a menu, the photo is sent over an encrypted connection to our processing service and then to our AI provider (OpenAI) to read the dishes. The photo is used only to produce your result. It is not stored on our servers after the request completes, is not used to build a profile, and is not tied to your identity. Our AI provider does not use API data to train its models.
  • A random device identifier. We send a randomly generated device ID with each request solely to prevent abuse (rate limiting). It is not linked to your name, email, Apple Account, or any advertising identifier.
  • Your goal and allergen preferences, and saved picks. These are stored only on your device. We never receive or store them on our servers.

What we do NOT do

  • No account or sign-up.
  • No advertising and no ad identifiers.
  • No analytics or third-party tracking SDKs.
  • No selling or sharing of personal data.

Third parties

We use two service providers strictly to deliver the app's core function:

  • Our processing service (Vercel). Receives the scan request and forwards the image to the AI provider. It does not retain the image after the request.
  • OpenAI. Reads the menu image and returns the analysis. Under OpenAI's API data policy, content sent through the API is not used to train its models.

We require these providers to handle data with the same protections described in this policy. We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any party for marketing or tracking.

Data retention and deletion

  • Menu photos: processed in real time and not stored after your result is returned. There is no photo history on our servers.
  • Device identifier: held transiently only for short-window rate limiting; it is not stored in a durable profile.
  • On-device data (goals, allergens, saved picks): kept only on your device. You can delete all of it at any time from You → Your data → Clear my data, or by deleting the app. Because we do not keep this data on our servers, there is nothing server-side for us to delete.

Withdrawing consent

You can stop all data processing at any time by not scanning, by revoking camera access in iOS Settings, or by deleting the app. Clearing your data in the app removes everything stored locally.

Permissions

  • Camera. Used only to capture the menu in front of you. You can instead pick a photo from your library; that uses Apple's system photo picker and the app only receives the single image you choose.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details. Manage or cancel a subscription anytime in your App Store settings.

Health information

MenuIQ provides estimated nutrition information and conservative allergen flags to help you ask better questions. It is not medical advice and cannot guarantee accuracy or safety. Always confirm allergies and ingredients with restaurant staff.

Children

MenuIQ is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and post the new version at this URL.
