Privacy

Last updated June 29, 2026

MenuIQ reads restaurant menus and ranks dishes for your goal. We collect almost nothing. Here is everything the app touches.

What we handle

Your menu photo — sent encrypted to read the dishes, then discarded. Never stored, profiled, or tied to you.

A random device ID — sent only to limit abuse. Not linked to your name, email, or any ad identifier.

Your goal, allergens, and saved picks — stored only on your device. We never receive them.

Who processes a scan

Vercel forwards the image and keeps nothing. OpenAI reads it and returns the result; under its API policy that content is not used to train models. Neither shares your data with advertisers or brokers.

What we never do

  • No account or sign-up
  • No ads or ad identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No selling or sharing of your data

Your data, your control

Clear everything stored on your device anytime from You → Your data → Clear my data, or by deleting the app. Since we keep nothing on our servers, there is nothing for us to delete. Revoke camera access in iOS Settings to stop scanning.

Subscriptions

Payments are handled by Apple. We never see your payment details. Manage or cancel anytime in your App Store settings.

Not medical advice. Nutrition figures are estimates and allergen flags are conservative cues to help you ask, not guarantees. Always confirm with restaurant staff. MenuIQ is not directed to children under 13.
